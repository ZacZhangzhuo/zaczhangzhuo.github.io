Section Clipper



该项目是课程设计的一部分，设计本身为模块化叠合的混合功能住宅。

The project is part of the studio design and the design itself is a modular stack of mixed-use houses.


为了清楚地展示设计单元的内部环境、空间和整体结构，我制作了一个基于Unity 3D的增强现实程序：Section Clipper。用户可以通过这个程序加深对人类活动以及人与空间关系的理解。

To clearly show the internal environment, space, and overall structure of the unit, an augmented reality program, Section Clipper, based on Unity 3D has been developed. Users can deepen their understanding of human activity and the relationship between people and space through this special program.



软件设计 Software Design


首先，为了衔接一系列设计，代表设计形式的模型在Rhinoceros 3D软件上被建立。

Firstly, to bridge the series of designs, models representing the design form are built on the Rhinoceros 3D software. 


Rhino中的设计模型

Design models in Rhino3D


其次，所有的模型随后被导入Unity 3D进行进一步编辑。编辑的主要目的是将不同的模型元素按逻辑分组。例如，需要切割的建筑元素（墙、地板等）被分组，而不会被切割的元素（人、家具等）被分组。然后，工作的主要平台转移到Unity 3D。同时，这些复杂的模型通过合并算法和网格三角化算法被简化为简易模型。

Secondly, all models were then imported into Unity 3D for further editing. The primary purpose of the editing was to group the different model elements together logically. For example, architectural elements that need to be cut up (walls, floors, etc.) are grouped together, while elements that will not be cut up (people, furniture, etc.) are grouped together. The main platform for the work then moved to Unity 3D. Also, these complex models are reduced to low-poly models by merging and triangulation algorithms.


Unity3D 设置

Unity3D Settings


第三，EazyAR的主要设置将在Unity 3D平台上实现，如图像和模型位置的对应关系，以及追踪图像的数量。这一系列的设置在很大程度上取决于模型的复杂性和计算机的性能，当然也有其他相关的AR产品作为参考。经过几轮的调试，最终的参数被确定下来。

Thirdly, the main setting of EazyAR will be implemented on the Unity 3D platform, such as the correspondence between image and model positions and the number of images to track. This series of settings is strongly dependent on the complexity of the model and the computer's performance, although there are other related AR products as references. After several rounds of debugging, the final parameters were determined.

第四，为了实现“动态剖切”的效果，Unity ShaderLab被引入。这个程序最初是为游戏领域的设计师定制角色或场景的材质准备的，使其具有独特的显示效果或材质。ShaderLab是一种声明性语言，你在着色器源文件中使用。它使用嵌套括号的语法来描述Shader对象。ShaderLab 使用命令来设置GPU的渲染状态，然后再执行一个着色器程序或执行涉及另一个Pass的操作。为了更容易地编辑ShaderLab，Visual Studio Code被引入作为代码编辑器。

Fourthly, the Unity ShaderLab was introduced in order to achieve the ‘dynamic section’ effect. This program was initially intended for designers in the gaming field to customize the material of characters or scenes to give them a unique display effect or material. ShaderLab is a declarative language that you use in shader source files. It uses a nested-braces syntax to describe a Shader object. ShaderLab Uses commands to set the render state of the GPU before it executes a shader program or to perform an operation involving another Pass.  For editing ShaderLab more easily, Visual Studio Code is introduced as the main editor.


PS. 

Shader Language 诞生的目的是“尽可能的控制渲染过程”同时“利用图形硬件的并行性，提高算法效率”（参看：https://zhuanlan.zhihu.com/p/47433678）。目前Shader Language主要有三种：

  1. 基于 OpenGL 的 OpenGL Shading Language，简称 GLSL;

  2. 基于 DirectX 的 High Level Shading Language,简称 HLSL;

  3.  NVIDIA 公司的 C for Graphic，简称 Cg 语言。

但Unity ShaderLab的语言是与之上的语言相近的、基于C语言的、独特的语言（有点绕口）。但实现“剖切”的核心就是告诉渲染器，模型中的某些部分不显示，因此又绕不开这个关于图形渲染的部分。小z花费了很多时间学ShaderLab的语言和原理，也走了不少弯路，最终通过Github上大神的算法和自己的改动完成了这个特定的剖切shader的编程。在企图避开ShaderLab的尝试中，小z也想过用平面直接切割模型，用切过的模型渲染，但运行速度十分的慢。基于GPU的shader优势明显。

交互界面 Interactive Interface


软件的交互式界面包括以下主要内容：

1) 主摄像机屏幕。显示相机拍摄的实际图像和Unity渲染的模型。这是用户关注的主要部分。

2) 性能信息和系统信息。这个信息是为了适应不同设备的使用和调试的兼容性。它只存在于Beta版本中。

3) 标志和版权。显示版权和其他基本信息。

4) 摄像机开关。专门用于在设备上连接有其他摄像头时切换输入摄像头。

5) 显示家具。控制是否会显示家具模型。

6) 显示人物。控制是否显示人物模型。

7) 退出。点击退出应用程序。用户也可以按键盘上的'Esc'键退出。

The interactive interface of the software includes the following main elements：

1) Main camera screen: Displays the actual image captured by the camera and the model rendered by Unity. This is the main part of the user's attention.

2) Performance information and system information: This information is intended to accommodate the use of different devices and debugging compatibility. It only exists in the Beta version.

3) Logo and copyright: Display Copyright and other basic information.

4) Camera switch: Specially designed to switch the input camera if other cameras are connected to the device.

5) Display furniture: Control if the furniture models will be shown.

6) Display people: Control if the people models will be shown.

7) EXIT: Click to exit the application. Users can also press 'Esc' on the keyboard to exit.

硬件设备 Hardware Requirements


硬件设备是AR程序的一个重要基础。除了一台带摄像头的电脑，程序的主要硬件是几张纸。它们的作用是定位模型在现实空间中的位置和断面的位置。

Hardware devices are an important basis for AR programs. Apart from a computer with a camera, the main hardware for the program is several sheets of paper. Their role is to locate the position of the model in real space and the position of the section plane (Clipper).

总结 Review


与传统的空间设计领域相比，AR带来了更多的互动性。用户实实在在地在物理空间中活动，这样用户可以加深对人类活动和人与空间的关系的理解。技术的进步将在我们的物理世界上叠加多个虚拟世界，人类的生活将被无止境地扩展。

AR brings more interactivity compared to the traditional realm of spatial design. Users are literally moving around in physical space so that users can deepen their understanding of human activity and the relationship between people and space. Advances in technology will superimpose multiple virtual worlds on our physical world, and human life will be expanded endlessly.


说点人话：Unity其实还挺友善的，毕竟已经有比较完善的社区和API。EazyAR也非常友善易用。但是ShaderLab不友善，VSCode里甚至没有比较优秀的ShaderLab代码提示插件或者其他插件。关于一个问题的卡壳往往要花费很多时间解决。不过所幸还是做出来了，希望能给你带来启发。
